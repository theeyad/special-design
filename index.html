2022

The Idea

It started with a simple frustration. Too many apps, too many platforms, too many places to look. There had to be a better way. So we asked ourselves — what if everything you loved lived in one place?

The Dream Takes Shape

A small team of passionate storytellers, gamers, and dreamers came together with one shared vision. No investors. No big office. Just people who believed entertainment deserved to feel personal.
2023

Built For You

We spent a year listening. To fans, to creators, to everyday people who just wanted to enjoy what they love without the noise. Every feature we built came from you.
2024

We Go Live

The doors opened. What started as an idea in a small room was now a home for millions of stories, worlds, and moments. The response was overwhelming. You came. You stayed. You made it yours.

Growing Together

New films. New games. New voices. We expanded our world because yours kept growing. Every update, every addition — driven by the community that made us who we are.
